# Claim Boundary

## Allowed claims

- This repository accompanies an AI-assisted independent research manuscript on active particle systems with information feedback control.
- The model is an ABP-type numerical prototype with local variance minimization of internal attributes.
- Simulations show qualitative finite-size dependence, a susceptibility peak at low noise, and stronger long-range spatial correlations at moderate noise than at extreme low noise in the tested configuration.
- The interpretation is kinetic-arrest-like fragmentation and noise-induced annealing within the reduced numerical model.

## Forbidden claims

- This is an experimental validation of active matter.
- This proves a universal phase transition.
- This is a rigorous theorem of kinetic arrest.
- This validates biological, social, economic, or civilization-scale dynamics.
- This is a crowd-control or swarm-control technology.
- This is a safety-certified control framework.
- This is a complete hydrodynamic theory.

## Standard short wording

> This work is a reduced numerical prototype of ABP-like active particles with information feedback control. It suggests kinetic-arrest-like behavior and noise-induced annealing in the tested model, but it does not prove universal active-matter physics or validate real biological, social, or engineered systems.
